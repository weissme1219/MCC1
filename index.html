About Us

Founded in 1922, McHenry Country Club has been a premier destination for golf enthusiasts and social gatherings for nearly a century. Our mission is to provide our members with an exceptional experience through our top-notch facilities, events, and services.

Membership

Join McHenry Country Club and enjoy exclusive benefits, including access to our championship golf course, dining facilities, and social events. We offer a variety of membership options to suit your needs.

Apply for Membership

Golf

Our 18-hole championship golf course offers a challenging and enjoyable experience for golfers of all skill levels. With beautiful scenery and well-maintained greens, it's a golfer's paradise.

Events

McHenry Country Club hosts a variety of events throughout the year, from golf tournaments to social gatherings. Our facilities are also available for private events, such as weddings and corporate meetings.

Dining

Enjoy exquisite dining at our club's restaurant. Our menu features a wide range of delicious dishes prepared by our talented chefs. Whether you're looking for a casual meal or a fine dining experience, we have something for everyone.
